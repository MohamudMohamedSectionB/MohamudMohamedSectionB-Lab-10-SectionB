Los Angeles Lakers

Los Angles Lakers is professional Basketball team founded in 1947 and is one of the most successful NBA teams in history with 17 NBA Championships.

List of NBA titles Lakers held

1949, 1950, 1952-1954, 1972, 1980, 1982, 1985, 1987-1988, 2000-2002, 2009-2010, 2020.

History of Lakers

  1. First founded in 1947
  2. First originated in Minneapolis and was known as Minneapolis Lakers
  3. It was moved to Los Angeles in 1960
  4. They made 32 NBA Finals Apperance
  5. They are tied with the Boston Celtics for most Championships
